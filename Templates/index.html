About Us

MediQ is an AI-powered health-assist chatbot designed to provide users with information about potential conditions based on their symptoms. With a user-friendly interface, MediQ helps users understand their health concerns quickly and efficiently. While it does not offer medical reminders or wellness tips, it serves as an initial point of guidance, empowering users with relevant health insights.
